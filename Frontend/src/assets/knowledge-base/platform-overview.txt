# Raqmiya Platform Overview

## What is Raqmiya?
Raqmiya is a comprehensive digital marketplace platform that connects creators with customers worldwide. Our platform specializes in digital products, online courses, software, templates, and various digital services.

## Platform Features

### For Customers
- **Digital Product Marketplace**: Browse and purchase digital products from verified creators
- **Secure Payment Processing**: Multiple payment methods with secure transactions
- **Instant Downloads**: Get your purchased products immediately after payment
- **Review System**: Read and write reviews for products you've purchased
- **Wishlist**: Save products for later purchase
- **Library**: Access all your purchased products in one place
- **Customer Support**: 24/7 support for all your needs

### For Creators
- **Creator Dashboard**: Manage your products, sales, and earnings
- **Product Upload**: Easy-to-use interface for uploading digital products
- **Analytics**: Track your sales, views, and customer engagement
- **Payment Processing**: Receive payments monthly with transparent fee structure
- **Marketing Tools**: Promote your products to our customer base
- **Creator Support**: Dedicated support for creators
