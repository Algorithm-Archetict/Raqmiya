# Raqmiya Platform FAQ

## General Questions

### What is Raqmiya?
Raqmiya is a digital marketplace platform that connects creators with customers. It allows creators to sell digital products, courses, and services while providing customers with a secure and user-friendly shopping experience.

### How do I create an account?
To create an account, click the "Register" button on the homepage. You'll need to provide your email address, username, and password. After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email.

### How do I reset my password?
To reset your password:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Follow the instructions in the email

## Creator Questions

### How do I become a creator?
To become a creator:
1. Register for an account
2. Complete your profile with creator information
3. Submit required documentation
4. Wait for approval (usually 24-48 hours)
5. Start uploading your products

### What types of products can I sell?
You can sell various digital products including:
- E-books and guides
- Online courses
- Software and tools
- Templates and designs
- Audio and video content
- Webinars and workshops

### How do I upload a product?
To upload a product:
1. Log in to your creator dashboard
2. Click "Add New Product"
3. Fill in product details (title, description, price)
4. Upload your product files
5. Set availability and pricing
6. Submit for review

### How do I get paid?
Payments are processed monthly. You'll receive payments for all sales made in the previous month, minus platform fees. Payments are made via bank transfer or PayPal.

## Customer Questions

### How do I purchase a product?
To purchase a product:
1. Browse the marketplace
2. Click on a product you're interested in
3. Read the description and reviews
4. Click "Purchase" or "Add to Cart"
5. Complete the checkout process
6. Download your product immediately

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- Credit and debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers
- Digital wallets

### Can I get a refund?
Refund policies vary by creator. Most creators offer:
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Partial refunds for technical issues
- No refunds for digital downloads (after download)

### How do I download my purchased products?
After purchase:
1. Go to your "Library" section
2. Find your purchased product
3. Click "Download"
4. Save the file to your device

## Technical Support

### I can't log in to my account
Try these steps:
1. Check your email and password
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support if issues persist

### The download isn't working
If downloads aren't working:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable any download blockers
4. Contact the creator or support

### How do I contact support?
You can contact support through:
- Email: support@raqmiya.com
- Live chat (available during business hours)
- Help center: help.raqmiya.com

## Platform Features

### What is the rating system?
Products are rated from 1-5 stars based on customer reviews. Only verified purchasers can leave reviews to ensure authenticity.

### How do I leave a review?
To leave a review:
1. Purchase a product
2. Go to your library
3. Find the product
4. Click "Write Review"
5. Rate and write your review

### Can I message creators?
Yes, you can message creators through the platform's messaging system. Go to the creator's profile and click "Message Creator."

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

## Pricing and Fees

### What are the platform fees?
Platform fees are:
- 10% for creators on basic plan
- 5% for creators on premium plan
- No fees for customers (except payment processing fees)

### Are there any hidden costs?
No hidden costs. All fees are clearly displayed before purchase. The price you see is the price you pay.

### Do you offer bulk discounts?
Bulk discounts are available for certain products. Check individual product pages for bulk pricing options.

## Account Management

### How do I update my profile?
To update your profile:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Settings"
3. Click "Edit Profile"
4. Make your changes
5. Save

### Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account in settings. Note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### How do I change my email address?
To change your email:
1. Go to Settings
2. Click "Change Email"
3. Enter your new email
4. Verify the new email address

## Security

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?
To enable 2FA:
1. Go to Security settings
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Follow the setup instructions
4. Use an authenticator app to scan the QR code

### What should I do if my account is compromised?
If your account is compromised:
1. Change your password immediately
2. Enable two-factor authentication
3. Contact support
4. Review recent activity

## Mobile App

### Is there a mobile app?
Yes, Raqmiya has mobile apps for iOS and Android. Download them from the App Store or Google Play Store.

### Can I use the same account on mobile and web?
Yes, your account works seamlessly across all platforms.

### Are all features available on mobile?
Most features are available on mobile, though some advanced creator tools may be limited to the web version.
